Dragon Warrior Quest 🐉

Game Design by Colette

📜 Quest Rules
  • The Goal: Reach exactly Tile 100 to defeat the Dragon! Overshooting bounces you back.
  • Portals & Traps: Magic Portals 🌀 jump you forward. Dragon Traps 🔥 drop you backward.
  • Jewel Shield: Spend 💎 Jewels x 3 automatically to shatter a trap and stay safe!
  • Milestones: Passing tiles 25, 50, and 75 yields Treasure Chests containing 💎 Jewels x 1.
  • Hero Classes: Every Hero has a unique ability. Pick your favorite or Randomize!
